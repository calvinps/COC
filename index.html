About Us

Who We Are

Christians on Campus at the University of Michigan is a campus group comprising believers in Christ from various backgrounds. We love the Lord Jesus and endeavor to give Him the first place in all things. Col. 1:18

We welcome anyone who would like to pursue the truth hidden within the depths of the Bible regardless of their religion or background.

Our Goal

It is vital to have Christian companions throughout our college years to build, preserve, and strengthen our faith. With so much going on around campus, it is hard to give even a minute of our time to the Lord. Together, we want to recover the focus of our human life, which is to enjoy God and His presence!

How sweet it is to be able to pursue and experience this loving God! Phil. 1:14

Our Faith

We hold the faith which is common to all the believers
Titus 1:4; Jude 3

1

The Bible

The Bible is the complete divine revelation inspired word-by-word by God through the Holy Spirit.
2 Pet. 1:21; 2 Tim. 3:16

2

God

God is uniquely one yet triune - the Father, the Son, and the Spirit.
1 Tim. 2:5a; Matt. 28:19

3

Christ

The Son of God, even God Himself, was incarnated to be a man by the name of Jesus Christ.
John 1:1, 14

4

The Work of Christ

Christ died on the cross for our sins, shedding His blood for our redemption. Christ resurrected from among the dead on the third day. Christ ascended to the right hand of God to be Lord of all.
1 Pet. 2:24; Eph. 1:7a; 1 Cor. 15:4; Acts 1:9; 2:33, 36

5

Salvation

Whenever any person repents to God and believes in the Lord Jesus Christ, he is regenerated (born again) and becomes a living member of the Body of Christ.
Acts 20:21; John 3:3; Eph. 1:22-23; Rom. 12:5

6

Christ's Coming Again

Christ is coming again to receive His believers to Himself.
1 Thes. 2:19

Events

Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly
Col. 3:16a

Bible Studies

Join a weekly Bible study with a group of students and club staff to enjoy the Word of God together.

Weekend Dinners

Come over for home-cooked meals, singing, and fellowship in a stress-free setting. We meet on campus or in the nearby homes of families who support the club.

Sunday Worship

Sundays, 10am-12pm
2401 Plymouth Rd, Ann Arbor, MI 48105
Turner Senior Resource Center

One-on-One Appointments

Can't make it to the Bible studies? Don't like to study in large groups? Contact us to schedule a companion Bible study appointment with a fellow believer.

Small Group Bible Study

Like to fellowship in smaller groups? Contact us to be grouped in threes and fours for more personal fellowship.

Day Outings

Come along for the day as we explore nearby adventures such as you-pick apple farms, corn mazes, cider mills, and ski resorts.

Announcing the Good News

We also like to go out and meet our fellow students on the UM campus and share Christ as the gospel.

Retreats and Conferences

Once or twice each semester we will go on a weekend retreat to a conference featuring a guest speaker, singing, and the enjoyment of Christ with other students from Michigan and the entire Midwest.

Annual College Training

Attend the College Training and receive much help and supply in the matters of truth, life experience, gospel preaching, and character development that will equip you to be a shining testimony of the Lord in your college years and beyond!
