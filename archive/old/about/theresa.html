Once I Thirsted

Once I thirsted for a fountain
Something deep that would satisfy
But the fountain that I drank from
Left me searching and still so dry.
Then one day I found Him,
He satisfied my thirst,
Became in me a deeper source
Whence living waters burst.
Now I drink Him!
Hymn 1327

          This was my experience. I was a believer in the Lord Jesus Christ but still I was searching for something. I wandered from church to church with my list of specific requirements, looking for a place to call my home. Then I realized that what I was really searching for was something that couldn't be found in a group of people. What I was searching for was in Christ. He was my home. My wandering ceased. Only He could satisfy. Only He could quench my thirst. What I had been looking for in people, I found instead in Christ. Now I am in the local church, enjoying Christ together with the saints. The difference is that I don't look for perfection from them. I don't look for them to care for me. Instead, we together look to Christ to satisfy all our needs. And He does! Hallelujah! To anyone who's reading this, if you're a believer or not a believer, I encourage you with this stanza from the same hymn:

Empty wanderer drop the striving –
Isn't worth all the troublin';
Open up your heart believing,
Call "Lord Jesus" and He'll come in!
Then today you'll find Him,
And calling on His name,
You're joined to Him, you're one with Him,
You'll never be the same.
You won't want to!
