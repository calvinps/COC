If we really know the life of the Body, we will see that a Christian cannot live one day without the Lord Jesus, and neither can he live one day without other Christians.
Watchman Nee

 

Jesus Christ is so rich! We could never fully appreciate Him by ourselves. Therefore, it is vital for us to come together to share our enjoyment of Him with one another by singing, praying, reading, and fellowshipping. 

Throughout the week, we get together in different ways: we gather together in groups as small as twos and threes to groups of up to thirty  

This is what we are finding out more and more in our meetings. We love to share and receive each other's weekly portion of Christ. Only then can we actually begin to realize and appreciate the unsearchable riches of our Savior. And what a joy and encouragement this is to our spirit!
